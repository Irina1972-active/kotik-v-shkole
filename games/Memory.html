📖 How to Play

Game Rules:

  • Take turns flipping two cards to find matching animal pairs
  • When you find a match, answer the grammar quiz
  • Correct answer: +1 point and keep your turn
  • Wrong answer: No points, turn passes to other player
  • Player with most points wins!

Quiz Tips:

  • Use "is" for singular (he, she, it, this, that)
  • Use "are" for plural (we, you, they, these, those)
  • Use "am" only with "I"
